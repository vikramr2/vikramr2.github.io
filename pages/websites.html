Enter: Manjula V. Tekal

Manjula V. Tekal is not only my mom, but a very talented author.

She has a number of official translations of renowned works in Kannada, a commonly spoken South Indian language. Her translations help rejuvenate and retell stories that were once only accessible in this language.

Not only does she have translations, but she has her own original novel: Devayani.

It would be greatly appreciated if you check out her website (created by me) and explore her work!
